This program implements a solution to producer-consumer problem which is:
Box makes boxes but not more than 30
candymaker makes candies but no more than 30
wrappingpapermaker makes papaer to erap but not more than 4
candyandpaperconsumer consumes 1 candy and 3 paper to make 1 wrapped candy
Complete box takes 1 box and puts 5 wrapped candies in it and make 1 complete box.

All factories are working in a synchronized but but are not not dependent on each other.
